2017/08/29

Dewey

It was Dewey (1938), who was a great researcher and an inspiration for sociologists and psychologists. But why was he that important? Only time will tell. Bourne (2016) elaborates on various topics, unrelated to Dewey.

Hall et al. (2008) is an inproceeding bib entry.

And Sousa (2010) an inbook bib entry.

The FT (2017) comes up with a try to compare MBA schools. Which it really shouldn’t, as this tries to create a normative value system about what’s a good MBA degree.
